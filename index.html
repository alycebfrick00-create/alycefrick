Strategic book marketing built on data, not guesswork

I help authors find their readers

For over a decade, I've specialized in identifying why well-written books sit undiscovered while mediocre ones thrive. The answer is rarely quality. It's almost always positioning.

I work with authors across genres who need more than generic marketing advice. Through deep research into review patterns, category positioning, reader behavior, and algorithmic signals, I uncover the specific gaps keeping your book invisible.

My approach is calm, respectful, and data-driven. No hype. No empty promises. Just honest analysis of what's working, what isn't, and exactly how to fix it.

I came to book marketing through frustration. After working in digital analytics and SEO, I watched talented author friends struggle to get discovered. The marketing advice they received was either too generic or outdated. I started applying the same research methodologies I used in tech to book discovery, and the results were immediate. I have worked with over 200 authors across genres. My background in data analysis gives me a different lens than traditional book marketers. I focus exclusively on the technical positioning that determines whether algorithms and readers can find your book.

Because your readers exist. They just don't know you do yet.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? +

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Book Discovery Analysis and Strategic Repositioning typically take 2 weeks. More comprehensive projects may take 3-4 weeks depending on catalog size.

Do you work with self-published and traditionally published authors? +

Yes, I work with both. The strategies differ slightly based on your publishing path, but core research and positioning principles apply universally. Self-published authors typically have more control over implementation.

What information do I need to provide? +

Your book title, ASIN or ISBN, current sales links, any existing marketing materials, and a brief description of what you've tried. Everything else I can research independently.

What if marketing hasn't worked for me before? +

Most authors I work with have already tried various tactics without success. The issue is rarely effort. It's usually positioning, category selection, or metadata gaps that prevent discovery in the first place.

Do you guarantee specific results? +

I don't guarantee specific sales numbers because discovery depends on many factors. However, I guarantee my research will identify concrete, actionable gaps and that implementing changes will improve your discoverability.

What's the difference between the services? +

Book Discovery Analysis is diagnostic only. Strategic Repositioning includes diagnosis plus a complete action plan. Series Relaunch and Backlist Revitalization are for multi-book authors or older titles. Platform Building focuses on author brand. Launch Consultation is for pre-publication setup. Query Letter Review helps authors seeking traditional representation.
