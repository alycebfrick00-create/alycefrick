About Alyce

Strategic book marketing built on data, not guesswork

I help authors who write great books but can't find their readers.

For over a decade, I've specialized in one thing: identifying why well-written books sit undiscovered while mediocre ones thrive. The answer is rarely quality. It's almost always positioning.

I work with authors across genres, from debut novelists to backlist veterans, who need more than generic marketing advice. Through deep research into review patterns, category positioning, reader behavior, and algorithmic signals, I uncover the specific gaps keeping your book invisible.

My approach is calm, respectful, and data-driven. No hype. No empty promises. Just honest analysis of what's working, what isn't, and exactly how to fix it.

I came to book marketing through frustration. After working in digital analytics and SEO for traditional businesses, I watched talented author friends struggle to get their books discovered despite having quality writing and professional covers. The marketing advice they received was either too generic or based on outdated tactics. I started applying the same research methodologies I used in tech and e-commerce to book discovery, and the results were immediate. What started as helping a few friends turned into a specialized practice focused entirely on the mechanics of book discoverability. I have worked with over 200 authors across genres, from debut novelists to established midlist writers looking to break through plateau sales. My background in data analysis and consumer behavior research gives me a different lens than traditional book marketers. I do not do social media management, cover design, or ad campaigns. I focus exclusively on the technical positioning that determines whether algorithms and readers can find your book in the first place.

Because your readers exist. They just don't know you do yet.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks from our initial conversation to final strategy delivery. Book Discovery Analysis and Strategic Repositioning typically take 2 weeks. More comprehensive projects like Series Relaunch or Backlist Revitalization may take 3-4 weeks depending on catalog size.

Do you work with self-published authors, traditionally published authors, or both?

I work with both self-published and traditionally published authors. The strategies differ slightly based on your publishing path, but the core research and positioning principles apply universally. Self-published authors typically have more control over implementation, while traditionally published authors may need to coordinate changes with their publisher.

What information do I need to provide to get started?

You will need to provide your book title, ASIN or ISBN, current sales links (Amazon, Goodreads, etc.), any existing marketing materials or metadata, and a brief description of what you have tried so far. If you have multiple books, a complete list of your catalog is helpful. Everything else I can research independently.

What if I have already tried marketing and it did not work?

Most authors I work with have already tried various marketing tactics without success. The issue is rarely effort. It is usually positioning, category selection, or metadata gaps that prevent discovery in the first place. My approach identifies the specific mechanical failures preventing your book from being found, which is different from traditional marketing advice.

How do we communicate during the project?

All communication happens via email. You will receive regular updates at key milestones, and I am available to answer questions throughout the process. Once your strategy is delivered, you will receive a comprehensive document with all recommendations and can ask follow-up questions via email.

Do you guarantee specific results?

I do not guarantee specific sales numbers or rankings because book discovery depends on many factors beyond positioning alone, including book quality, cover design, and market timing. However, I do guarantee that my research will identify concrete, actionable gaps in your current approach, and that implementing the recommended changes will improve your discoverability compared to your current setup.

What is the difference between the services offered?

Book Discovery Analysis is diagnostic. It identifies what is wrong but does not include implementation strategy. Strategic Repositioning includes both diagnosis and a complete action plan for fixing category, keywords, and metadata. Series Relaunch and Backlist Revitalization are specialized versions for multi-book authors or older titles. Platform Building focuses on author brand and audience growth rather than individual book positioning. Launch Consultation is for pre-publication setup to ensure correct positioning from day one.
